# Business Insights Report: Campaign Performance

### 1. Project Objective
The goal of this analysis was to perform an Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on marketing campaign data. We tracked how users move through our sales funnel, calculated the exact Return on Investment (ROI) for each channel, and determined how to optimize our budget to increase profits.

---

### 2. Marketing Funnel Performance
We tracked user behavior across four major funnel stages to locate where potential customers drop off:

- **Impressions:** 12,823,250 views
- **Clicks:** 640,406 users clicked an ad (4.99% Click-Through Rate)
- **Leads:** 114,011 users signed up (17.80% Click-to-Lead Rate)
- **Conversions:** 38,926 users made a final purchase (34.14% Lead-to-Purchase Rate)

**Key Finding:** Our biggest drop-off happens right at the start, where 95.01% of people see our ads but don't click. We need to focus on improving our initial ad creatives and audience targeting to boost clicks.

---

### 3. ROI Analysis by Channel
We calculated the exact Return on Investment (ROI) percentage for each individual marketing platform:

- **Email Marketing (+1155.3%):** The absolute winner. It has incredibly low operational costs but generates high sales from interested users.
- **Affiliate Marketing (+197.9%):** A highly profitable channel with strong, steady returns.
- **Google Ads (+83.9%):** Brings in steady profit, though limited by high competitive bidding costs.
- **Social Media (-21.6%):** Operating at a net loss. We are spending more on social media ads than we are making back in sales.

---

### 4. Final Budget Recommendations

- **Reinvest heavily in Email & Affiliate:** Immediately shift underperforming ad spend into our high-yield Email Marketing and Affiliate pipelines to maximize revenue.
- **Cut Social Media Spending:** Scale down the Social Media ad budget by 30-40% to stop the financial loss. Pause underperforming campaigns and fix user targeting before spending more money there.
- **Maintain Google Ads:** Keep the current budget for Google Ads stable, but optimize search keywords to lower the cost per sale.
